पूर्वोत्तर भारत

Eastern Zone of India is rich in Cultural Heritage. You can have a taste of beaches, temples, monastries, buddhist circuit with a rural touch. Most Popular tourist destinations for this region are, Calcutta, Former Captal of India, Puri & Bhubaneshwar, Buddhist Circuit in Bihar, Monastries in Sikkim, Kanchenjunga Range of Mountains in Darjeeling etc.

2

उत्तर भारत

North India is a pure delight to mountain lovers and adventure enthusiasts. There are plenty of picturesque, popular, and off-the-wall hill stations nestled in the Himalayas.The places in this northern region can astound you by their cultural extravaganza, architectural marvels and local cuisine, all so different from each other yet sharing the same region.

3

दक्षिण भारत

South of India is the country’s haven of natural beauty abundant with mystical hills, intricately carved temples and quaint backwaters.It’s a melting pot of cultures, cuisines, traditions, and languages. There’s something to do for everyone here – water sports for the adventurous, backwaters for the romantics, trekking trails for hikers, and panoramic vistas for nature lovers.

4

पश्चिम भारत

West India is rolled down with the amber color of palaces, rustic flavor of historical India, sand dunes of the Rajasthan deserts, hippie culture of Anglo Indic Goa, the Western Ghats colored in the shades of green and white salt deserts of the Rann of Kutch. The topographical changes while traveling to western India is mirrored in its culture.
